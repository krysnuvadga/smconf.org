About SMConf

SMConf 2017 Is The Third Flagship Conference Of #SiliconMountain Which Attracts Creatives, Hackers And Organizers From Africa To Buea, Cameroon For A Day Of Code Labs, Talks And Sessions Of Inspiration And Fun.

Theme

Think Global, Build Local.

  • Featuring design workshops, code labs and ignite talks.
  • Everything required to challenge techies to conceive, code and collaborate.
  • Experience dedicated one-on-one sessions with startups at Startup Booths
  • Geeks showcase their talents; from good to great, iconic to inspirational.
  • We match off the map - pushing the boundaries of technology in Africa.
